Education

Forex Brokers

  • A broker is the intermediary giving retail traders access to the global currency market
  • Brokers provide trading platforms, real-time pricing, and order execution
  • Brokers earn through fees, spreads or commissions

A-Book vs. B-Book

A-Book Broker

Acts as agent. Earns from commissions/spreads, NOT from client losses.

✓ Transparent execution

B-Book Broker

Acts as counterparty. Takes opposite side of trades. Profits WHEN clients lose.

✗ Broker is the market

⚠ Around 90% of forex brokers are not licensed or regulated, making regulation a critical factor when choosing a broker.
