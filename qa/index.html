Q1
AFM Holland ja FMA Austria on avaldanud hoiatused TAG Marketsi kohta. Mis on olukord? AFM Netherlands and FMA Austria have listed warnings against TAG Markets. What's the situation?
Regulatsioon

Hollandiga on juba lahendatud. Me suhtlesime AFM-iga, selgitasime et TAG ise ei turunda otse Hollandis. Hetkel räägime FMA Austriaga. Kui regulaator avaldab hoiatuse, me lõpetame selles riigis turundamise ja räägime otse regulaatoriga, et kõik on raamatu järgi.

Netherlands is already resolved. We communicated with the AFM, told them TAG itself doesn't market directly in the Netherlands. Currently in talks with FMA Austria. When a regulator issues a warning, we stop marketing in that country and talk directly with the regulator to show everything is by the book.

Q2
TAG on Mauritiuse FSC litsentsiga. Mida see tähendab EL kodanikele? Kas on kaitse kui midagi juhtub? TAG is FSC Mauritius licensed. What does that mean for EU members? Is there protection if something goes wrong?
Regulatsioon

Jah, kaitse on olemas. Kui TAG läheb pankrotti, saad läbi regulaatori oma raha tagasi nõuda. Regulaator kaebab omanikud kohtusse sinu raha pärast. Kliendi kapital on ka kindlustatud.

Yes, you have protection. If TAG goes bankrupt or illiquid, you can claim your funds back through the regulator. They will sue the owners for your money. Client funds are also insured.

Q3
Kas plaanite soetada EL litsentsi? Are you planning to get an EU license?
Regulatsioon

Tegelikult me juba ostsime Küprose litsentsi, aga otsustasime mitte kasutada. EL regulatsioonide all me ei saaks maksta partneritasusid ega pakkuda 12x/24x võimendust. See on meie põhitoode, seega me ei läinud seda teed.

We actually already bought a Cyprus license but decided not to use it. Under EU regulation we can't pay affiliate commissions or offer 12x/24x amplified accounts. That's our core product so we chose not to go that route.

Meil oli ühe tunni jooksul 200 kuni 300 negatiivset arvustust. Tõime üle partnerid teiselt maaklerilt, kes siis ründasid meid. Tegeleme Trustpilotiga, et need ükshaaval maha võtta, tõestades et tegemist pole päris klientidega.

We had 200 to 300 negative reviews posted within one hour. We brought over affiliates from another broker who then attacked us. We're working with Trustpilot to remove them one by one, proving each review is from someone who isn't actually a client.

Q5
Millal saab raha välja kanda ja mis on reeglid? When can you withdraw and what are the rules?
Väljamaksed

Kasumit saad välja kanda igal ajal, samal päeval. Esialgse deposiidi 12x/24x kontodel saad välja 30 päeva pärast. Tava ja zero kontodel esialgse kapitali igal ajal. Lisatingimusi ei ole.

Profits you can withdraw any time, same day. Initial deposit on 12x/24x accounts after 30 days. Standard and zero accounts, initial capital any time. No hidden rules or extra conditions.

Q6
Millisel juhul TAG keeldub väljamaksest? Under what circumstances would TAG reject a withdrawal?
Väljamaksed

Ainult siis kui klient kuritarvitab süsteemi — arbitraaž, manipulatsioon või tegevused mis lähevad meie tingimuste vastu manuaalse kauplemisega. Kui kasutad kopeerimislahendust nagu SONIC, siis see sind ei puuduta. Kopitreidid käivad alati reeglite järgi.

Only if a client abuses the system through arbitrage, manipulation, or activities against our terms with manual trading. If you use copy trading like SONIC, this doesn't apply to you. Copy traders always trade within the rules.

Q7
Kes tellis võltsarvustused ja kas saab parandada? Who ordered the fake Trustpilot reviews and can it be fixed?
Usaldusväärsus

Googlest leiab teenuseid mis müüvad 300 kuni 1000 negatiivset arvustust 5000 kuni 6000 dollari eest. Me palusime Trustpilotil reitingu maha võtta kuna see on ebaõiglane. Esitame tõendeid et need pole päris kliendid ja need võetakse ükshaaval maha.

You can Google services that sell 300 to 1000 negative reviews for $5,000 to $6,000. We asked Trustpilot to take down the rating because it's unfair. We're providing proof that these aren't real clients and they're removing them one by one.

Q8
Kas väljamakset saab teha pangakontole? Ühel kliendil lükati tagasi. Can withdrawals be made to a personal bank account? One client says his was declined.
Väljamaksed

Väljamakse meetod peab kattuma deposiidi meetodiga. Pangast sisse — pangale välja. Kryptost sisse — kryptole välja. See on rahapesu tõkestamise nõue.

Withdrawal method must match deposit method. Bank deposit means bank withdrawal. Crypto deposit means crypto withdrawal. This is anti-money laundering compliance.

Q9
Kui tihti Mauritiuse regulaator kontrollib TAG Marketsit? How often does the Mauritius regulator check TAG Markets?
Regulatsioon

Iganädalaselt. Meil on igal nädalal kõne. Nad kontrollivad meie kontosid, raamatupidamist, et kliendi kapital on segregeeritud ja et kõik klappib. Peamine prioriteet on kliendi raha eraldatus ettevõtte ja partnerite rahast.

Weekly. We have a call every week. They check our accounts, bookkeeping, that client funds are segregated, and that everything balances. Their top priority is client funds being separate from company and affiliate funds.

Q10
Miks ei ole TAG kodulehel likviidsuspakkujaid nagu Vantage'il? Kuidas on 2-aastasel maakleril 75 LP-d? Why aren't liquidity providers listed on TAG's website? How does a 2-year-old broker have 75 LPs?

Vantage'il ja StarTraderil on palju rohkem kui 14–25, nad lihtsalt ei näita kõiki. Meil on palju sest me teeme palju A-book voogu. Ma olen 11 aastat selles valdkonnas ja tunnen kõiki. Spetsiifiliselt AITECH kogukonnale on meil leping et kõik on ainult A-book — me oleme kliendi poolel, teenime tehingutelt mitte klientide kaotustest. Paneme mõned LP nimed kodulehele üles.

Vantage and StarTrader have way more than 14–25 LPs, they just don't list them all. We have many because we do a lot of A-book flow. I've been in the game 11 years and know basically everyone. For the AITECH community specifically we have a contract that everything is A-booked only — we're on the client's side, we make money from transactions not from clients losing.
