Part 1
Registration & KYC Verification
~5 minutes
1
Register on the AITECH IB Portal
Click the registration link provided by your referrer and fill in the registration form: first name, last name, email, password, country and phone number. Check both risk disclaimer boxes and click Register.
Next: Verify email
2
Verify Your Email (IB Portal)
You will receive an email from the IB Portal. Click the "Verify Account" button to confirm your email address.
Prev Next: Email verified
3
Email Verified
After clicking the link, you will see an "email Verified" confirmation message. Click "continue To Login".
Prev Next: Log in
4
Log In to the AITECH Portal
Enter your email and password and click "Login".
Prev Next: Register TAG Markets
5
Register Tag Markets from the IB Portal
After logging in, scroll down until you find the "Tag Markets Registration" banner. Click "Register TagMarkets".
Important! Do not register directly on the Tag Markets platform – it must be done through the IB Portal so that the accounts are synchronized.
Prev Next: Sync account
6
Sync Your Account
A popup "Welcome to TAG Broker" appears. Optionally enter a password and click "Sync Account".
Prev Next: Syncing
7
Syncing in Progress
Wait while the system verifies your information and synchronizes the accounts.
Prev Next: Sync successful
8
Sync Successful
A "Sync Successful!" confirmation is shown. TAG Broker will send you an email with further instructions. Click "Close".
Prev Next: Confirm TAG email
9
Confirm Your Email on TAG Markets
Open the TAG Markets email. It contains your login credentials and a "Confirm Email Address" button – click it to activate your TAG Markets account.
Prev Next: Dashboard
10
TAG Markets Dashboard
You are now on the TAG Markets dashboard. You will see a notice at the top to complete KYC verification.
Prev Next: Start KYC
11
Start KYC Verification – Sumsub
Click the "upload documents" link on the dashboard. A Sumsub verification popup appears. Click "Continue".
Prev Next: Start verification
12
Start Verification
Click "Start verification". The process consists of two steps: providing an identity document and completing a liveness check.
Prev Next: Document type
13
Select Document Type
Select the issuing country and document type (ID card, passport, etc.), then click "Continue".
Prev Next: Liveness check
14
Liveness Check
Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the liveness check. Wait while the system verifies your submission.
Prev Next: KYC verified
15
KYC Verified
"Your profile has been verified" message is shown. KYC has been successfully completed – you can now make deposits.
Prev Next: Dashboard after KYC
Part 2
Making a Deposit
~5 minutes
16
TAG Markets Dashboard After KYC
After KYC approval, your trading account is visible on the dashboard (MT5, account no. 50627155). Click "Deposit" to make a deposit.
Prev Next: Deposit amount
17
Select Deposit Amount
Select the currency (USD) and enter the desired amount (e.g. $777) or choose a preset amount ($100, $500, $1000). Click "Next".
Prev Next: Payment method
18
Select Payment Method
Choose your payment method: Crypto (Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT, etc.) or card/bank transfer (Visa/Mastercard). Click your preferred method.
Prev Next: Confirm crypto
19
Confirm Crypto Payment
If Crypto is selected, the deposit amount ($777) is shown. Click "Submit" to proceed.
Prev Next: Choose crypto
20
Select Cryptocurrency
Choose a cryptocurrency: Bitcoin, Ethereum, Tether (USDT), USD Coin, Binance Coin or Solana.
USDT (Tether) is recommended due to low transaction fees.
Prev Next: USDT network
21
Select USDT Network
For USDT, select the network: it is recommended to choose BSC BEP-20 due to low fees.
Prev Next: Send payment
22
Send Payment via QR Code or Address
A payment address and QR code are shown. Open your crypto wallet and send the exact amount (777.225395 USDT) to the displayed address. The payment expires in ~15 minutes.
Prev Next: Payment detected
23
Payment Detected
The system detects your payment and shows "Payment found! Awaiting network confirmations". Wait for network confirmations.
Prev Next: Find CopyX
Part 3
Connecting to the SONIC CopyX Strategy
~5 minutes
24
Find CopyX and Browse Traders
On the dashboard, click "CopyX" in the left menu. The "Browse Pro Traders" page opens showing all active strategies. Find and click on the "SONIC" strategy.
Prev Next: SONIC details
25
SONIC Strategy Details
Review SONIC strategy stats: ROI +57.99%, Max Drawdown 0.26%, Win Rate 88.82%, 61,426 followers, Minimum investment $10. Click "Connect".
Prev Next: Copy Strategy
26
Copy Strategy – Enter Investment Amount
The "Copy Strategy" window opens. Enter the investment amount (min $10). Account type is automatically set to "Amplify".
Important! If the manager loses more than 5% of the starting balance, your account will be liquidated.
Prev Next: Confirm investment
27
Copy Strategy – Confirm Investment
The "Copy Strategy" window shows your entered amount. Verify the details before proceeding to the next step.
Prev Next: Community Token
28
Enter Community Token #DW
Click "Community Token" and enter the token code #DW, then click "Verify".
The token changes your Amplify account drawdown limit to 5% of your starting balance.
Prev Next: HASH & Copy Now
29
Enter HASH/GU-ID and Click Copy Now
After token verification, the HASH/GU-ID field appears. Enter 1000 and click "Submit". Check both consent boxes and click "Copy Now".
Prev Next: Account created!
30
CopyX Account Created!
"CopyX Account Created" message is shown. Click "Continue". The SONIC strategy will now automatically copy the trader's trades to your account.
Prev Finish
